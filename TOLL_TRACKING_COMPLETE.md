## 💰 HOW TOLL COSTS ARE CALCULATED:

### **Truck Toll Multipliers:**
```

Tandem (2 axles):  $4.00 per toll (2.0x)
Tri-axle (3 axles): $5.00 per toll (2.5x)  ← Your default
Quad (4 axles):     $6.00 per toll (3.0x)
```

### **Example Calculation:**

**Route: Philadelphia to Allentown**
- Toll plazas: 2 (PA Turnpike entry + exit)
- Tri-axle truck toll: $5.00 × 2 = $10.00 one way
- **Round trip toll cost: $20.00**
- Added to haul rate automatically

## 🎯 HOW IT WORKS:

### **When You Calculate a Route:**

1. **User enters address**
2. **System finds route** with Valhalla
3. **Valhalla detects tolls** on the route
4. **System calculates truck toll cost** based on axle count
5. **Toll cost added to breakdown**
6. **Final rate includes tolls**

### **Toll Avoidance Option:**

**"Use Tolls (Faster, costs extra)"**
- Allows toll roads
- Faster route
- Higher cost (tolls included)
- Best for: Time-sensitive deliveries

**"Avoid Tolls (Slower, saves money)"**
- Routes around toll roads
- Longer distance/time
- Lower cost (no tolls)
- Best for: Cost-sensitive quotes

## 🚀 REGIONAL TOLL KNOWLEDGE:

### **PA Turnpike (I-76, I-476)**
- Northeastern Extension (I-476)
- Main line (I-76)
- Class 9 trucks (tri-axle): ~$2.50-5.00 per toll
- **Avoidable** via US-22, US-222, local roads

### **NJ Turnpike (I-95)**
- Runs entire length of NJ
- Most expensive tolls in region
- Class 9 trucks: $5.00-10.00 per toll
- **Often worth avoiding**

### **Atlantic City Expressway**
- To/from South Jersey
- Moderate tolls
- Few alternatives in that area

### **Delaware Memorial Bridge**
- I-295 crossing
- $12.50 for tri-axles
- No free alternative for this crossing
